Frequently Asked Questions

Blue Lock is an anime where young strikers—including Yoichi Isagi, Meguru Bachira, Rensuke Kunigami, and Hyoma Chigiri—enter a ruthless program led by Ego Jinpachi.
300 strikers are brought together and compete in various stages. Those who fail are eliminated and banned from representing Japan forever.
Jinpachi Ego created Blue Lock after Japan's failure in the World Cup, believing that Japan needs a world-class striker to compete globally.
